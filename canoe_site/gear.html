Essential Gear

  • Personal Flotation Device (PFD) — required for safety.
  • Paddle — correct length matters (torso + canoe width).
  • Proper clothing — quick-dry layers and sun protection.
  • Whistle, bailer, spare paddle, and first aid kit.
